# Battery Alert Troubleshooting

## Low Battery Threshold
A low-battery alert is generated when measured voltage drops below `11.8V` for a sustained reading window.

## Common Causes
- overnight parasitic draw
- poor grounding
- aging vehicle battery
- device continues reporting while the engine is off for extended periods

## Practical Interpretation
Repeated low-battery alerts at similar overnight times often indicate the asset is parked with a weak battery or ongoing draw.

## Follow-Up Checks
1. Compare alert times across days.
2. Review the minimum observed battery voltage trend.
3. Inspect ground and power wiring if the pattern persists.
4. Ask whether the asset has been inactive for extended periods.
