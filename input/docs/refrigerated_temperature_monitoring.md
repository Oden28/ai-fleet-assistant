# Refrigerated Temperature Monitoring

## Expected Operating Range
For this exercise, refrigerated assets are expected to remain between `2C` and `8C` under normal operation.

## Interpretation Rules
- Brief spikes may occur during loading or door-open events.
- Sustained readings above `8C` are more concerning than isolated brief spikes.
- If only daily averages are available, you may identify suspicious assets but cannot prove excursion duration.

## Practical Guidance
When summarizing temperature issues:
- distinguish between mild concern and likely actionable concern
- explain limits of the data
- avoid claiming food safety impact unless duration is known
